MICA GD Blog

Most of the process was spent analyzing the classes that Ben setup and seeing how I could style them to fit what I thought the site needed. From interviewing other students I found that legibility was a large concern and that they wanted to do away with the current layout.

Looking back on my approach I feel like I did not really accomplish what I hoped for the site as a whole. All of the pages are similiar in appearance so the experience is unified and feels connected. But they are so similiar that the different sections may not be easily distinguished.

If I had the chance to re do the assignment I would focus on each page as a different experience rather than getting each to feel similiar.

I do think that I got what I needed to learn out of this class. And that was learning how to learn. Before with writing code I would not know where to begin but now I am armed with resources that really explain in depth how things funtction and how to troubleshoot and deconstruct pages to learn from them.
